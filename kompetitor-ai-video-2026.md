## Ringkasan Strategis

### Kategori Berdasarkan Fitur Utama

**Text-to-Video Generators (High Quality)**
- Sora, Runway, Luma, Kling, Veo, Pika — *berlomba di kualitas sinematik dan realism*

**Avatar & Talking Head**
- HeyGen, Synthesia — *dominan di corporate video, training, internal comms*
- Descript — *hybrid: avatar + text-based editing*

**All-in-One Production Pipeline**
- InVideo AI, Canva — *dari script ke publish, paling cepat & mudah*

**AI Video Editing (Browser-based)**
- Kapwing, VEED — *fokus pada subtitle, trimming, social media repurposing*

**Specialized Repurposing**
- Opus Clip — *khusus long-form ke short-form*

**Creative Cloud Ecosystem**
- Adobe Firefly — *terintegrasi dengan Premiere Pro, After Effects, Photoshop*

### Tren 2026
1. **Native audio** menjadi standard (Sora 2, Veo 3.1, Kling 2.6)
2. **Multi-model aggregation** (InVideo bundling Sora+Veo, Adobe Firefly 30+ models)
3. **Unlimited generation** mulai diadopsi (Adobe Firefly, kompetitor menyusul)
4. **Agentic AI** (HeyGen Video Agent, Runway Agent, Canva AI 2.0 conversational)
5. **Consolidasi pasar** kecil ke platform besar
6. **Credit-based pricing** masih dominan, tapi mulai bergeser ke flat-rate unlimited
